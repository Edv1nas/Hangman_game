Welcome to Hangman Game!

Hangman is a simple word guessing game. Players try to figure out an unknown word by guessing letters.

If too many letters which do not appear in the word are guessed, the player is hanged (and loses).

In this game you have 10 bad guesses, if you use all 10 guesses you will be hanged and lose.

But if you guess the hidden word without using up all the attempts you will win the game and be the CHAMPION.
